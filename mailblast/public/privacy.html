1. Data We Process

MailBlast processes account, session, and campaign activity data required to authenticate users, send emails, and run analytics in the admin panel.

2. Data We Store

  • Account profile and authentication identifiers needed for login continuity.
  • Session and access activity details such as login/logout events and timestamps.
  • Email campaign analytics data such as send counts, status, and delivery outcome logs.
  • Technical metadata used for security, monitoring, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.

3. Why We Store It

  • To keep your account authenticated and secure.
  • To provide app analytics and admin-level performance tracking.
  • To maintain service quality, auditability, and platform integrity.

4. Retention and Security

Stored records are retained for operational and analytics purposes and are protected using reasonable technical and organizational safeguards.

5. Policy Changes

If these privacy rules change in the future, this page will be updated before new behavior is applied.
