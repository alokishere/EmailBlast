What Problem We Solve

  • Manual Gmail sending is too slow for campaigns with many recipients.
  • CC/BCC methods risk privacy and look unprofessional.
  • Many bulk tools require SMTP setup and steep learning curves.
  • Teams need a fast way to send from their own trusted Gmail identity.

How MailBlast Works

  1. Click Get Started and sign in with Google.
  2. Create subject and message body (plain text and optional HTML).
  3. Add recipients and optional file attachments.
  4. MailBlast sends one email per recipient through Gmail API.
  5. You get clear sent/failed status for every run.
